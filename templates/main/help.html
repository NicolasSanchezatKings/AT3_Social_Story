Help & Support

  1. Create an Account: Sign up with your email address to get started
  2. Write Your First Story: Click "Create Story" and use our guided editor
  3. Preview and Save: Use the preview feature to review your story before saving
  4. Manage Your Stories: View, edit, or delete stories from your dashboard

Key Principles:
  • Use "I" statements: Write from the first person perspective
  • Be specific: Include concrete details about what, when, where, and how
  • Stay positive: Focus on what to do rather than what not to do
  • Keep it simple: Use clear, age-appropriate language
  • Include feelings: Describe emotions and reactions
Structure Example:

Context: "When I go to the library..."

Behavior: "I will use my quiet voice..."

Reason: "This helps others concentrate..."

Feeling: "I feel proud when I follow library rules."

Common Issues:
Can't save my story:

Make sure both title and content are filled in. Stories need at least 10 characters of content.

Lost my work:

The app auto-saves drafts. Check if there's a "restore draft" option when creating a new story.

Can't find my stories:

Make sure you're logged into the correct account. Stories are private to each user.

Forgot my password:

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.
