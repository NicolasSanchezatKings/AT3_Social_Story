What are Social Stories?

Social stories are short descriptions of social situations that help individuals understand and respond appropriately to various social contexts.

  • Easy to understand
  • Personalised content
  • Practice social skills
  • Build confidence
Learn More
Tips for Writing Stories
  • Be Specific: Use clear, concrete language that describes exactly what happens.
  • Use "I" Statements: Write from the perspective of the person who will read it.
  • Stay Positive: Focus on what to do rather than what not to do.
  • Keep it Simple: Use age-appropriate language and short sentences.
