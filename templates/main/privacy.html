Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Social Stories collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Story Data: Content you create, edit, or upload within the app, including text, images, and templates.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as page visits, interactions, and device information.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Social Stories application.
  • To personalize your experience and save your progress.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support, and new features.
  • To maintain security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide core app functionality (e.g., cloud storage, AI services).
  • Third-party services (such as Gemini AI) may process data as part of their integration. Please refer to their privacy policies for details.

4. Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Your password is securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Access to your stories is restricted to your account unless you choose to share or publish them.

5. Your Rights

  • You may access, edit, or delete your account and stories at any time.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related requests or concerns.

6. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified to you.

7. Contact Us

  • If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@socialstories.app.
